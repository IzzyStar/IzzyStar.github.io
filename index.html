About Me

Hello! I am Isabel Schumann majoring in Graphic Design and minoring in Psychology. I am from the Midwest, where I live with my parents and my adorable dog Noel. With my creative work, I love to include deeper meanings, colors, and playful themes.
I have designed various things for print including brochures, letterheads, and posters. I have designed several logos and page layouts. I even designed this website. :)
As a photographer, I specialize in fine art. I enjoy expressing ideas in my work, light painting, and using formalistic elements as the main part of my photos.
